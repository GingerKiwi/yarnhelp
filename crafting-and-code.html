Crafting and Code Coffee Chats

Welcome to the Crafting and Code Coffee Chats!

  • What are Crafting and Code Coffee Chats? They are monthly online meetups on Discord for web and software developers who also do any type of crafts.
  • What's the vibe? They're a social, relaxed way to unwind, meet other developers, and share your love of any type of crafting or art.
  • What types of crafts count? Is it only knitting and crochet? (Any kind of crafting is awesome!): knitting, crochet, weaving, cross stitch, woodworking,scrapbooking, sewing, drawing, or anything else crafty or artsy!
  • I'm just learning to code/am switching careers. Do I have to be working in tech? Any career stage of dev is welcome - from starting self-teaching or postsecondary courses, to senior devs. We welcome UX design/and product professionals that like coding too!
  • Do I have to be good at a craft? I'm just learning to (knit, draw, make puppets, ...)Again, as long as you're into anything crafty or arty you're welcome. It doesn't matter if you're new, or have been doing your craft(s) since you were a kid.
  • When is the first Crafting and Code Coffee Chat?: The first one is tentatively scheduled for March 18th, 2023 at 6:30pm EDT.
  • Where are they? The Coffee Chats will be on a discord video channel on a new server.
  • What will be the regular day of the month for the Crafting and Code Coffee Chats?The virtual coffee chats will be held on the 3rd Tuesday of every month. Depending on interest and where active discord community members are a mid-day Sunday one may be added so crafty devs in other time zones (B.C., California, UK,... !).
