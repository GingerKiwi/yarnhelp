How Much Yarn?
The Yarn Gift Helper

Instructions

Make sure that the yarn you're going to buy is medium weight/thickness. The label will have an yarn ball icon like the one above that says "4" and/or say, worsted weight, medium weight, aran, or rarely "afghan". Type the number of yards per ball or skein of yarn, and click the calculate button to get the number of balls/skeins you should buy as a gift.
